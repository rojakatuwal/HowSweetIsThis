About Us 

                           Hershey Company, often called just Hershey or Hershey's,
                                  is an American multinational confectionery company
                             headquartered in Hershey, Pennsylvania, United States, 
                         which is also home to Hersheypark and Hershey's Chocolate World.
                         The Hershey Company is one of the largest chocolate manufacturers in 
                      the world;it also manufactures baked products, such as cookies and cakes, 
                         and sells beverages like milkshakes, as well as other products.
